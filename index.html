Sorter for Color Rise Harmony (Holofes6) Performances.

Filter by Stages and/or Branches if you'd like, exclude certain types of performances if you'd like, then hit Start at the top of the page.

Hover over a checkbox for a tooltip with additional information about what it does.

When sorting, select the performance you like better from the two options. You can hit Tie if you like them equally, or don't remember either performance. If you made a mistake, you can hit Undo to go back one choice.

You can save your progress by hitting "Save Progress", and then storing the URL for later. Your browser cookies should also automatically save your progress (Hit "Load Autosave").



This is a merge sort, so you will be selecting one performance out of two, hundreds of times. This might take you a while, especially if you sort all 106 performances.

Performances with multiple songs in them (medley) are treated as a single performance, indicated by "->" in the text.

Some of the images of multi-talent performances are a bit scuffed, since finding a good frame with all talents visible was quite challenging. Apologies.



Keyboard controls during sorting: H/LeftArrow (pick left) J/DownArrow (undo) K/UpArrow (tie) L/RightArrow (pick right) S (save progress).

Before sorting: S/Enter (start sorting) L (load progress).

1/2/3 always correspond to the first/second/third buttons.
